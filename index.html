Explore, Experience, Empower.

To travel is to embark on a journey with no definite outcome, with the understanding that no matter where you go, you will come back changed. You might find parts of yourself in the corners of a bustling city, or pieces of your soul hidden in quiet landscapes far from home. Travel teaches you that sometimes, the most meaningful destinations are the ones you never expected to find

Destinations


Darjeeling

A heritage hill city in West Bengal, also known as the Queen of the Hills, famous worldwide for its tea, Darjeeling Himalayan Railway and the magnificient view of Mt Kanchenjunga.

View Details
Darjeeling Offbeat

Good for those looking to spend time in the midst of the nature. Enjoy the scenic views of tea plantations, orange plantations, pine forests, flower gardens, etc.

View Details
Kalimpong

A city in Westbengal, mainly famous for the colonial heritage. People mainly like to explore the villages and offbeat places around this city

View Details
North Sikkim

Lachen and Lachung are the two main places in this region famous for the views of snow capped Himalayan mountains, frozen lakes and valley of flowers.

View Details
Silk Route

Covers several places in the Eastern parts of Sikkim. Famous for the winding roads of the old silk route and the offbeat places along the way.

View Details
Pelling

A beautiful city in western Sikkim. It is mostly known for beautiful monasteries, Rabdentse ruins, bird sanctuary, Kanchenjunga waterfalls.

View Details
Pelling Offbeat

Explore the offbeat locations in western Sikkim near Pelling. Includes locations like Chayatal, Rinchenpong, Yuksom and stays in homestays or farmhouses in the midst of nature.

View Details
Dooars

Explore the wildlife sanctuaries and forests around Dooars. Embark on jeep or elephant safaries through the jungles enjoy the sightings of wild animals in their natural habitat.

View Details
Sandakphu

For the adventure seekers, trek through the Singalila National Forest and enjoy the 360 degree views of the tallest mountains in the world. Can be explored via both trek or car.

Kashmir

Explore the heaven on earth. Enjoy the beautiful vallyes of Sonmarg, Gulmarg, Pahelgum and many other places. Every season offers unique and spectacular views of region.

Ladakh

Explore the ture beauty of the Himalayas with the mesmerizing views of landscapes and rivers.

Shimla Manali

One of the best hill destinations that offers the view of snow capped mountains and lush greenary.
